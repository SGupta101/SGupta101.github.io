The Process

I took this photograph for our 2019 December photo issue. For the December photo issue I designed two spreads centered on the topic of highs and lows. In these two spreads, I played with idea of emotional highs and lows and literal highs and lows in terms of camera angle. This particular picture was taken from a bird's-eye-view.

This is the spread that the photograph was ultimately placed in.
