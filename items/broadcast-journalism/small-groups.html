About: As we entered the pandemic, it was not only COVID-19 that was on the rise. Adolescent mental health has been left unaddressed for years. This article uses Tableau data visualizations to take a closer look at national depression and anxiety statistics over time compared to my school county’s depression-related funding.

To explore data journalism, I learned how to use the powerful data visualization software Tableau. This is the first article I constructed using this software. I spent one month garnering this data from the U.S. Census Bureau, SocialPro, and my school county’s budget documents. I also conducted a fifteen minute interview with our school’s social worker, Ms. Danielle Armstrong. Finally, I drew the featured image using Faber-Castell pens, and added text using Adobe Photoshop.
