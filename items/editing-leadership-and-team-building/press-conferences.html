Press Conferences

Every month, we hold a press conference with the Jefferson principal Dr. Ann Bonitatibus. During these conferences, we ask Dr. Bonitatibus a series of questions in relation to our 3-page print newsmagazine cover story.

The following are soundbites from each of the press conferences that I have led.

Our October cover story centers around the effect of the pandemic on Jefferson staff and students with mental health issues and learning disabilities.

Our November cover story centers around two students who coded software that allows people to request a mail-in-ballot.

Our February cover story centers around the effect of the pandemic on school sports.
